Justin Swaney

45 Carleton Street E25-353 · Cambridge, MA 02142 · jswaney@mit.com

I am a graduate student at MIT interested in creating better tools for studying how the brain works. I am working on 3D printing artificial vascular systems to improve neural organoids as a biological model of the human brain. I am detail-oriented yet pragmatic, and I value being able to explain complex concepts simply.
